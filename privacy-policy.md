# Privacy Policy for TipWise

Last updated: May 3, 2026

## Overview
TipWise is a tip calculator app developed by SwiftApps Studio. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection
TipWise does NOT collect any personal data. The app:
- Does not require account creation
- Does not collect names, emails, or contact information
- Does not track your location
- Does not share any data with third parties
- Does not contain ads

## Data Storage
All data (saved bills and folders) is stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this data.

## Children's Privacy
TipWise does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
